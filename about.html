About Me

I'm Sheila Soh — an undergraduate Applied Computing (Fintech) student at the Singapore Institute of Technology, passionate about designing digital experiences that are both functional and meaningful. My work bridges the creative and the technical: from developing Java-based educational games and Flutter stock trading apps to designing accessible web platforms with Firebase and PHP.

These roles sharpened my ability to deliver UI/UX solutions, streamline backend processes, and align product features with real-world user needs.
